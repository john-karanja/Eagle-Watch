Our Team

Our key strength at EWG is in the selection of recruits, the vetting process, training and remuneration of the guards. We emphasis on Certificate of Good Conduct issued by the National Security Intelligence Service (NSIS)to ensure that those we employ have no criminal record. We also carry out our internal background checks of all potential candidates to ensure that there are no records of professional or ethical misconduct on any of them before we employ them.

Training of guards

The training of the guards is conducted by qualified and equipped officers in the security field. We have ex-military and police force trainers of disciple (drill) and the other related areas. The other specialized areas are taught by tutors trained in those areas.
