Our Services

Our Team

Our key strength at EWG is in the selection of recruits, the vetting process, training and remuneration of the guards. We emphasis on Certificate of Good Conduct issued by the Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI) to ensure that those we employ have no criminal record. We also carry out our internal background checks of all potential candidates to ensure that there are no records of professional or ethical misconduct on any of them before we hire them.

Training of Guards

Training of the guards is conducted by qualified and experienced officers in the security field. We have ex-military and police force trainers of discipline and the other necessary areas. Other specialized areas are taught by tutors trained in those disciplines.

Personnel training scope covers the following

Drill and Discipline

Sentry Duties

Patrolling

Surveillance

Crime detection and deterring

Preliminary Investigations and Report Writing

Public Relations

Communication Skills

Risk Management

Radio Communication

First Aid

Fire fighting

Counter terrorism

Training completion

It is only upon completion of the training and qualifying that recruits pass out and are deployed to assignments. The morale and effectiveness of our guards is closely observed and includes the early attention to all their issues at all levels of our organization. We ensure that they are well equipped and adequately prepared to perform their duties at all times. This is accomplished through our experienced supervisors largely recruited from disciplined forces.

Where we are going

The main drivers of the security industry are diverse, dynamic and will include

Economic and GDP growth in Kenya

Heavy capital and real estate investments

Increased levels of conflict, crime and threats to security

Technological changes and need for enhanced surveillance

Increased levels of unemployment

What we have done

EWG has therefore taken a critical look at the challenges facing the general public and businesses and enhanced both skill and technology to offer diverse and proven solutions and expertise to customers that meet their short and long term private security concerns allowing them time to focus on their core businesses.
