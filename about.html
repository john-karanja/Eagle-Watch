Who we are

Eagle Watch Group has been primarily in the private security services business over the last three decades operating in Nairobi, the Rift Valley, Central, Northern, Western and Eastern Regions of Kenya and offering guarding and canine services to commercial and industrial premises, factories, residential properties and gated communities. Over the years the company has grown and found it necessary to reach the customers more closely and therefore got into two brands operating under different companies within the Group - Chancery Security Company Ltd and Batian Security Company Ltd.

Chancery Security Company Ltd was incorporated in March 2002 under the Kenyan Companies Act Cap 486. It started off with just over 100 guards and has grown over the years to the present establishment in several areas of the country with its headquarters in Nairobi. It has branch offices in Nairobi and Thika. The company has a guard force of several hundred guards and growing as the business acquires more assignments.

Batian Security Company Ltd based in Nyeri was incorporated in February 2003 under the Kenyan Companies Act Cap 486. It started off within the Mount Kenya Region with about 100 guards and has grown to cover Nyeri, Karatina, Embu, Keroguya and Meru and part of the Eastern region with an establishment of several hundred guards.
